Mobilising higher education for the transition to carbon neutrality

Round-table

UCLouvain
Louvain-la-Neuve
Tuesday 14th of June
From 9:00 till 12:30

Register Agenda
Following the publication of its report, Education4Climate and its partners, The Shift.be, the Belgian branch of the Sustainable Development Solution Network (SDSN) and the Shift Project are organising a roundtable with representatives from Belgian universities and High Schools to discuss how to make Belgium's higher education programs a key enabler of the carbon neutrality transition, adapting their content to better include all aspects of environmental sustainability.
